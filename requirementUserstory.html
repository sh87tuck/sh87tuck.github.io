Requirement Summary

In alignment with extreme programming practices all requirements are based on use cases and user stories.
The prime user story used by our team in analysis for the DiscoveryCRM is User Story 1.

User Story 1
Actor: Placement Agent
Use Case: Profile Handling

John is a recruiter working at a staffing consulting company called PeopleSearch.
John’s co-worker Adam works at the same company as system administrator. When Adam receives descriptions about an open position from an account manager he creates a job requirement in a database.
He can post new job requirements to PeopleSearch’s web site and popular online job boards.
If a job seeker applies to the newly posted job opening, DiscoverCRM’s parsing module parses the resume and reconcile module will check the job candidate application data for likely duplication.
The system will alert Adam so he can send the parsed resume to the recruiter John.
when John starts his day, he may have candidates from Adam. After he calls all candidates from Adam, he searches more possible candidates in DiscoveryCRM’s database.
If he runs out of all candidates then he searches online job boards’ resume database.
When John finds a good fit, he parses the resume into DiscoveryCRM.
Parsing module parses the resume and shows possible duplication based on new resumes attributes, currently name and email.
John can decide update existing profile if he finds the resume is already existed or create a new profile. Even though he created duplication,
A.I. checker will find duplicates and merge the two profiles.
